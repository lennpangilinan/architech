Welcome my portfolio

Began my Public exposure in the field of Architecture and Construction. I Designed/Conceptualized the proposed project, present my sketches and final presentations drawings to the client or the Government Agency involved. Upon approval of the scheme I expound further my responsibility by presenting a reliable cost estimate, material specifications basing on my Architectural and Engineering Utilities Designs. Upon approval of the budget for the project cost, I have to prepare the work schedule basing on the work program. A Building Permit Processing shall be done as soon as Plans and other documents are already prepared solely by Myself to speed up the approval from the Building Officials of the certain area or place where the project shall be Implemented. As this documents presented, I have completed THE DOCUMENTATION PHASE of the project. The second phase of the duties and responsibilities as a Provincial Architect, I have to invite the PROSPECTED BIDDER/CONTRACTOR through a letter of invitation which I myself has to prepare and to officiate the bidding process as a Chairman of the Bidding Committee. As the date come to the Bids and Awards come, I have to send another letter informing the Winning Bidder that the project have been Awarded to them. And then, A Notice to Proceed will be served to the Bidding Winner. Stating the date on the Notice to Proceed comes the Ground Breaking of Project. After such activities comes my Project Implementation and Supervisions
